treadmill and the natural human tendency to quickly adapt to new living
standards > Wandering about the Mission in San Francisco pondering how the

environmental waste > Examining how marketing tactics might seek to trigger
personal desires (as with a carrot tied to a stick) > How might I personally
counter these tactics? And why might I want to?
